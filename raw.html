Jamaican Banana Fritters

This recipe is a homage to my culture and my long lasting sweet tooth. Try making this at home!

Ingredients

  • 4 large super ripe bananas or 1 ½ cup mashed bananas
  • 3/4 cup Whole Wheat flour
  • 3/4-1 cup All purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2-4 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 1 /2 teaspoon salt or more adjust to taste

Instructions

  1. Mash the bananas till it becomes a chunky paste
  2. add the dry ingredients to one bowl
  3. Add the wet ingredients to a different bowl
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the banana mixture
  5. slowly add the dry mix to the banana mix. A little at a time. Stir in the mix then slowly add more.
  6. slowly add the dry mix to the banana mix. A little at a time. Stir in the mix then slowly add more.
  7. slowly add the dry mix to the banana mix. A little at a time. Stir in the mix then slowly add more.
  8. slowly add the dry mix to the banana mix. A little at a time. Stir in the mix then slowly add more.
  9. slowly add the dry mix to the banana mix. A little at a time. Stir in the mix then slowly add more.
  10. Add oil to stove on medium heat
  11. Once oil is hot spoon in the incorporated mixture. into small dollops around the size of a mini pancake.
  12. Once edges are bubbling flip to cook on the other side
  13. After it is done frying place paper towl on a plate and place cooked fritters on to it.
  14. Let it cool and enjoy! :)
